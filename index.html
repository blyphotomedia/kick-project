Description


We are committed to save 1000 doctors' lives over the next 10 years!
Do you know that one third of UK healthcare professionals suffer from mental health disorder? And over 400 doctors took their lives between 2011 to 2015?

Doctors are honorable profession, they work really hard and they save lives! 360 Kick is the community project that bring back the healthy and fulfilling lifestyle to UK healthcare professionals through martial arts and mindfulness practices!

This workshop is designed to help busy healthcare professionals to release stress, form loving relationship with the body and gain resilience through combination of Taekwondo and mindfulness practices.

It encourages the participants to look after their health and wellbeing first, so they can be fulfilled with their career and spark more joy when being of service for others!

Our Team

Hannah Xu (Taekwondo Instructor)

Hannah is the owner of her business Xceptional Consultancy, that provides tax consultancy services to doctors. Despite her busy life, she has been living a healthy and active lifestyle for over 10 years.

She is a 2nd Dan Black Belt in Taekwondo and a registered British Taekwondo instructor. She started taekowndo in 2008 and she has committed to the path to black belt as it helped her to gain self confidence and find inner peace through the practice

She now creates this community project, 360 Kick, that aims to encourage doctors to participate in martial arts so help them release stress and they can help more people become healthier and happier!

Bhavna Mistry (Mindfulness and Meditation)

Bhavna is a 'Heart Centred Mentor' who teaches meditation, Qigong and other mindfulness practices. Bhavna started meditation and mindfulness practice over 30 years ago. She became Reiki Master and teacher since 2007.

Her 'heart-centred' approach is about going to the core of your heart to heal all hidden wounds and uncover your true soul path. This helps you find the place of deep inner peace and allows you to embrace love and compassion in life.

Julie Ly (Taekwondo Instructor)

Julie started practicing Taekwondo over 5 years ago and is a 2nd Dan black belt in WT Taekwondo. The martial art has helped her build her confidence, increase not only physical but mental strength and taught her self defense skills.

Julie thrives in helping others develop these benefits and loves to be apart of guiding others in their journey to achieving their desired, individual, goals through her passion.

Julie is now a registered and insured British Taekwondo instructor with a level 2 certificate in coaching Taekwondo.
